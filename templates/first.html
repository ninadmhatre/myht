    Untangle your deployments the easy way...

    We think deployments should be easy, configurable, auditable and standardised. Deployment should be one of first things to get right not an annoying afterthought in building your application. Any deployment should just fit in with the existing framework. You should be free to use the language of your choice. You should be free to deploy to multiple operation systems. Vamos was designed and built keeping all of this in mind.

    Design Principles

    Transparency

    Answers the 3 W's of audit and compliance. Who did What and When?

    Visibility

    Tracking the progress of deployments. See what was deployed where.

    Integration

    Reduce risk of lengthy migrations by integrating into existing release process.

    Simplicity

    Resist the temptation for complexity as developers cannot be bothered to learn yet another tool!

    Benefits

    Integrating Vamos into your current deployment process will reduce the cost and time in delivering software to your clients. Why waste time and effort in developing a solution when you can get Vamos Deploy?

    • Standardize release procedures
    • Reduce learning curve for staff
    • Release more reliably. Fewer failures
    • Release more frequently
    • Reduce production support
    • Free developers from supporting releases
    • Increase co-operation between Dev and Ops teams
    • Test more effectively and efficiently
    • Roll-back in no time at all
    • Track all activity with the full audit
